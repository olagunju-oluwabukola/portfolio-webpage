Title: Residential Development Project

Introduction:
As the Electrical Engineer for a prestigious residential development project, I played a pivotal role in implementing a comprehensive range of electrical and technology solutions to enhance the comfort, security, and connectivity of the living spaces. This multifaceted initiative included the installation of lighting systems, power sockets, automated switches, CCTV, video entry systems, internet connectivity, wireless access points, and cable TV infrastructure.

Scope of the work
The project aimed to provide residents with a modern and technologically advanced living environment. My responsibilities covered the design, installation, and integration of the following systems:

  • Lighting Systems
  • Power Sockets and Automated Switches
  • CCTV Systems
  • Video Entry Systems
  • Cable TV infrastructure
  • User Experience and Innovation
  • Internet and wireless access points
