Title: MEP Design Integration for a Three-Floor School Project, Oshogbo

MEP design for a three-floor school project, I ensured the implementation of comprehensive systems for heating, ventilation, lighting, plumbing, and fire protection. Challenges like space constraints and budget limitations were overcome through innovative design strategies. Quality assurance measures were applied to verify performance and compliance with safety standards. The project delivers a safe, comfortable, and sustainable learning environment for students and staff.
