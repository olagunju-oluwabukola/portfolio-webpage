Services

My service cut accross various section under MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) design engineering and also M&E Engineering.

MEP Design Engineering

MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) design engineering involves planning and coordinating the essential systems within buildings. This includes designing heating, ventilation, air conditioning, electrical, and plumbing systems to ensure comfort, safety, and efficiency. MEP engineers also focus on energy efficiency, compliance with regulations, and cost-effective solutions while integrating modern technologies into building systems

M&E Engineering

Building services design involves planning and coordinating essential systems within buildings for functionality, efficiency, and comfort. This encompasses mechanical, electrical, plumbing, fire safety, energy efficiency, automation, compliance, and cost considerations, ensuring safe, sustainable, and cost-effective building operations.
